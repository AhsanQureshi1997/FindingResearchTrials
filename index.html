UACOMP Active Trials Dashboard

This version uses the ClinicalTrials.gov v2 API and searches across several UACOMP-related aliases, then merges duplicate studies by NCT ID. Because public trial records may be filed under the medical school, Banner Phoenix, or broader University of Arizona naming, this dashboard is designed as a practical approximation of Phoenix-campus trial activity rather than a guaranteed official census.
